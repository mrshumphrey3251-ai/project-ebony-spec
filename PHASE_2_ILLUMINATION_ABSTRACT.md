## 1. MACHINE INTENT SIGNALING (REDACTED)
For heavy autonomous edge-rovers to operate safely alongside human personnel, the machine must visually project its kinetic intentions. Project Ebony integrates a full 360-degree illumination suite to communicate operational states.
* **Capability:** The edge-compute node governs forward visibility arrays, turning indicators, and hazard states entirely offline without requiring cloud-based fleet management software. 

## 2. AUTONOMOUS KINETIC BRAKING ILLUMINATION (REDACTED)
* **Execution:** The rover's braking indicators are not triggered by mechanical pedals or secondary sensors. They are directly woven into the core drive-by-wire algorithm.
* **Capability:** The moment the NLP logic engine calculates a deceleration slew rate or processes a hard-halt command, the system autonomously fires the rear illumination arrays, signaling an impending kinetic stop before the chassis even begins to physically slow down.

## 3. EDGE-COMPUTE RELAY ISOLATION (REDACTED)
* **Architecture:** To protect the neural processing cores from high-amperage draw, all illumination systems are physically decoupled from the logic boards.
* **Capability:** The Jetson Orin utilizes proprietary micro-voltage triggers to actuate salvaged solid-state relays, allowing the brain to safely control heavy 12V automotive lighting arrays while adhering to the strict Zero-Spend capital mandate.
