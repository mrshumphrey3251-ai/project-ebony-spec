# Biosecurity Triage & Containment Specification

This document outlines the localized containment protocols, airlock state tracking, and environmental sealing cascades for agricultural or site biological anomalies.

## 1. Automated Zone Isolation
* **Airlock Pressure Modulation:** Controls structural airlock sequencing natively via isolated SCADA relays to enforce negative pressure containment spaces automatically.
* **M2M Containment Cascades:** If an anomaly flag is verified, the local node broadcasts immediate lockdown commands directly to adjacent machinery and site perimeters over the sub-GHz mesh.

## 2. Waste Path Mitigation
* Actuates high-temperature isolation valves to prevent cross-contamination across localized water, fluid drainage, or ventilation channels.
