## 1. FACTORY INTEGRITY RETENTION (REDACTED)
To maintain the stringent safety baselines inherent to ADA-compliant mobility devices, Project Ebony utilizes a non-destructive hardware decoupling strategy.
* **Capability:** The machine's factory motor controller, heavy-amperage routing, and electromagnetic braking systems are left entirely intact. The optionally manned architecture does not replace the vehicle's brawn; it simply intercepts its communication.

## 2. SYNTHETIC TELEMETRY INJECTION (REDACTED)
* **Architecture:** The localized edge-compute core is wired directly into the physical operator interface (the steering tiller). 
* **Capability:** By algorithmically synthesizing the exact analog voltages produced by physical human input, the system "spoofs" the factory safety controllers. This allows the localized AI to flawlessly pilot the 24V chassis without triggering anti-tamper lockouts or sacrificing the vehicle's native emergency braking protocols.
