# Fire Suppression Control System Specification

This specification handles the high-speed thermal sensory monitoring, automated chemical deployment cascades, and hardware isolation protocols for electronics enclosures.

## 1. Automated Thermal Containment
* **Sensory Interrupt Loops:** Monitors optical flame detectors and localized temperature sensors via low-latency industrial networks to identify ignition points instantly.
* **Aerosol Suppressant Activation:** Directs high-priority digital instructions to solid-state relays to deploy gaseous fire suppressants into a compromised enclosure inside milliseconds.

## 2. Electrical Isolation Handshakes
* Instantly cuts off secondary charging circuits and primary power lines to the affected module to eliminate electrical ignition drivers during a fire event.
