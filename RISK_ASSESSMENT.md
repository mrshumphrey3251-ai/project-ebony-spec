# Autonomous Risk Assessment & Threat Modeling Specification

This document details the real-time probabilistic risk calculations, fallback threshold matrices, and localized decision trees executed natively by edge nodes.

## 1. Local Probabilistic Threat Calculations
* **Dynamic Risk Vectoring:** Continually maps environmental anomalies, sensor dropouts, and hardware telemetry faults against a localized Bayesian inference engine.
* **Cascading Failure Prevention:** Computes structural risk multipliers in real time to isolate failing mechanical blocks before degradation propagates to adjacent systems.

## 2. Autonomous Mitigation Boundaries
* When calculated risk indices breach pre-compiled safety margins, the node autonomously forces downstream hardware lines into high-security or low-emission operational profiles without waiting for operator intervention.
