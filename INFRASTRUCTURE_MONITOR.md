# Structural Infrastructure Monitoring Specification

This specification handles the long-term strain, tilt, concrete degradation, and foundational vibration tracking loops for facilities and defensive perimeters.

## 1. High-Resolution Strain & Tilt Ingestion
* **Vibrating Wire Sensor Interfaces:** Processes frequency inputs from structural strain gauges embedded in load-bearing columns via isolated analog-to-digital converters.
* **Seismic Waveform Profiling:** Runs localized Fast Fourier Transforms (FFT) on accelerometer arrays to classify structural micro-fissures or foundational shifts before failures manifest.

## 2. Threshold-Driven Safety Cascades
* Automatically drops heavy mechanical fire doors or isolates high-pressure facility lines if structural deformation limits cross safe architectural margins.
