## 1. DEMOCRATIZING ADA COMPLIANCE (REDACTED)
Project Ebony serves as a foundational proof-of-concept for next-generation accessibility tech. By combining offline voice actuation with localized 3D spatial awareness, the project demonstrates how legacy mobility platforms can be safely upgraded for operators lacking fine motor control. The architecture ensures 100% operational independence without requiring internet connectivity or subscription-based cloud APIs.

## 2. THE HARDWARE-AGNOSTIC THESIS (REDACTED)
The proprietary engineering within Project Ebony proves that the "Brain" and the "Brawn" of heavy machinery can be forcefully decoupled. The localized edge-compute logic utilized to safely navigate and govern a 24V differential-drive mobility chassis is completely hardware-agnostic, serving as a scalable blueprint for the autonomous retrofitting of large-scale agricultural and industrial assets.
