## 1. DYNAMIC STRUCTURAL SCALING (REDACTED)
Project Ebony’s Optionably Manned Vehicle (OMV) architecture is not rigidly locked to a single proprietary power plant. The modular canopy framework is designed to scale dynamically based on the verified output of the salvaged solar asset.
* **Capability:** The engineering pipeline mandates strict dimensional and electrical validation prior to chassis modification. This ensures that the structural weight of the environmental armor scales perfectly with the power-harvesting capability of the array, mathematically preserving the platform's Center of Gravity (C.G.) without adding unnecessary mechanical bulk.
