## 1. THE CENTAUR PARADIGM (REDACTED)
True operational versatility requires a platform that amplifies human capability when manned, and maintains operational output when empty. Project Ebony is designed as an Optionally Manned Vehicle (OMV).
* **Capability:** The localized edge-compute architecture utilizes physical occupancy tracking to seamlessly toggle the machine's behavior profile. When an operator is aboard, the system acts as a protective drive-by-wire co-pilot. When empty, it transitions into an independent autonomous logistics node.

## 2. DYNAMIC AUTHORITY SHIFTING (REDACTED)
* **Architecture:** The bare-metal vision and navigation pipelines dynamically reallocate their computational priority based on the presence of a human asset.
* **Capability:** In manned operations, the system restricts itself to crash-prevention and failsafe vetoes, preserving human sovereignty over the vehicle. In unmanned operations, the system assumes total vector-generation authority, executing complex navigational tasks while self-imposing strict kinetic speed governors to ensure environmental safety.
