# Environmental Isolation Specification

This document details the physical ingress protection, hermetic sealing parameters, and thermal barrier configurations for extreme field operations.

## 1. Hermetic Ingress Protection
* **IP67/IP68 Standards:** Structural enclosures housing core compute cores utilize dual-layer elastomeric gasket seals to prevent particulate and fluid infiltration at depth.
* **Desiccant Integration Matrix:** Internal atmospheres are conditioned via replaceable molecular sieve packets to maintain zero internal relative humidity and eliminate trace condensation during thermal transitions.

## 2. Thermal Barrier Topologies
* Utilizes vacuum-insulated panel barriers and hydrophobic aerogels to insulate native computing iron from intense external environmental swings.
