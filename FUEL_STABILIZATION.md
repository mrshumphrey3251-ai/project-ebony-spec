# Fuel Stabilization & Storage Quality Specification

This specification handles the long-term chemical monitoring, ambient condensation prevention, and filtration logistics for isolated hydrocarbon fuel vaulting.

## 1. Fuel Quality Telemetry
* **Dielectric Constant Analysis:** Gathers live capacitance data from fluid monitoring probes to immediately calculate changes in water content or fuel degradation.
* **Particulate Contamination Alerts:** Measures fuel clarity using localized optical turbidity sensors before fluid passes into active engine supply rails.

## 2. Automated Polishing Cascades
* Actuates localized filtration pumps to automatically circulate stored fuel through water separators and multi-stage particulate filters if moisture thresholds drop outside acceptable baselines.
