# EMP Mitigation & Electromagnetic Hardening Specification

This file outlines the structural isolation parameters, surge shunts, and topological layout designs required to protect edge node electronics from high-energy electromagnetic pulses.

## 1. Faraday Enclosure Topologies
* **Galvanic Continuity:** Specifies continuous conductive shielding profiles around core computing hardware blocks to completely ground transient electromagnetic fields.
* **Waveguide-Beyond-Cutoff Venting:** Implements sub-wavelength physical mesh geometries on all cooling and ventilation ports to block high-frequency energy infiltration while maintaining airflow.

## 2. High-Speed Transient Shunting
* Deploys gas-discharge tubes and ultra-fast TVS diodes directly at the physical boundary interfaces of external power and antenna lines to suppress high-voltage spikes in nanoseconds.
